About

自己紹介

I’m a software developer and QA tester living and working in Tokyo. My background spans testing and web development — which means I think about both how things are built and how they break.

Right now I’m a software tester at Oplus Inc., a Japanese SaaS company building shift and attendance management tools. I do exploratory testing, API testing, and bug reporting — working across English and Japanese with the engineering team.

Outside of work I’m building full-stack projects with React, TypeScript, Vue 3, and Python FastAPI — all deployed on AWS with CI/CD pipelines. I’m pushing toward a junior developer role and treating every project as real practice.

I hold a BSc in Computer Science and have lived and worked in Nigeria, the UK, Spain, and Japan. That kind of movement shapes how I communicate and adapt in multicultural teams.
